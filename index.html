Meet Walid Hakim

Reliable Leadership for South Carolina

Born to an American mother, and a Greek/Palestinian father. In 1985, his family moved to his mothers home state of South Carolina, where he attended Lexington High School and Newberry College. After Iraq invaded Kuwait in 1990, he dropped out of college and enlisted in the US Marine Corps. After the Marine Corps, Walid worked in a variety of different jobs; car sales, restaurant owner, police officer, political consultant, actor, to name a few. Now he is running to represent you!
