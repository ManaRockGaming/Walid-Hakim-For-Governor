PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 10/23/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how The Campaign to Elect Walid N. Hakim (“the Campaign,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information obtained through our website, digital platforms, and online services.

The Campaign may collect personal and non-personal information from users in various ways, including when individuals visit or interact with our website, fill out forms, make donations, subscribe to updates, volunteer, sign petitions, or contact the Campaign. Information collected may include names, email addresses, phone numbers, mailing addresses, payment details for donations, and any other data voluntarily provided. The Campaign may also automatically collect technical information such as IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.

Collected information may be used to communicate with supporters, volunteers, and donors; send updates, newsletters, and event details; process donations and maintain financial compliance; improve the website, outreach, and voter engagement efforts; and comply with applicable election and donation reporting requirements. The Campaign may also use aggregated, non-identifiable data to analyze website usage and campaign performance.

The Campaign may share information with trusted vendors or service providers who assist in operations, such as payment processors, email services, and web hosts. These partners are required to protect all information and use it only for authorized purposes. The Campaign may also disclose information when required by law or to comply with election reporting regulations.

We take reasonable measures to safeguard all collected information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no online system is entirely secure, and users submit information at their own risk. Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and improve functionality. Users may adjust browser settings to disable cookies, though some features may not function properly as a result.

Users may unsubscribe from campaign communications or update contact preferences at any time by following the instructions provided in campaign emails or by contacting the Campaign directly.

The Campaign reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. For questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is used, please contact:
